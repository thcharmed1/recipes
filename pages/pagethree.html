Green Bean, Potato, and Bacon Soup

Ingriedients

  • 1/2 lb of green beans
  • 1lb of bacon
  • 3 or 4 full size potatos
  • chicken broth
  • water/li>
  • salt, pepper, and garlic/li>
  • 1/2 yellow onion

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy and save the grease
  2. Add full box of chicken broth to crockpot and season with garlic,salt and pepper
  3. Add cut up potatos, onion, and green beansli>
  4. Add in Bacon and Bacon Grease
  5. Add water to the crockpot until it almost submerges all ingridients
  6. Cook on low setting for six to eight hours or high for 4 hours in slow cooker
