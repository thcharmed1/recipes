Recipe

Chile Mac

Ingriedients

  • Hamburger Helper Chili MacaroniC
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1 can Black Beans
  • Purple Onion
  • Jalapenos
  • Mexican Shredded Cheese

    Cooking Instuctions

  1. Brown the 1lb of Ground Beef
  2. Add in contents of the Hamburger Helper
  3. Add 1 3/4 cup of water
  4. Add Black Beans>
  5. Let the water come to a boil, lower heat to a simmer and cover. Let simmer for 8 to 10 minutes
  6. Cut up Onion and Jalapeno and add to the plate and top with Shredded Cheese"
